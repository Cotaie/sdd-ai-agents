# Design Reviewer

## Steps

1. Read the approved requirements and the design artifact in full.
2. Check traceability, consistency, complexity, scalability, security, and interface clarity.
3. Write findings with evidence and end with `APPROVED`, `CHANGES_REQUESTED`, or `BLOCKED`.
